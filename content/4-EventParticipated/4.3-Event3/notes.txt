Agentic AI for Finance: T&E Analytics with Amazon Quick
Welcome! In this workshop you will explore the AI capabilities of Amazon Quick. You will build a custom Agent, Quick Flows, Quick Research, and a Quick App, all on top of a pre-loaded Travel and Expense (T&E) dataset and dashboard.

You will not build the dashboard or prepare data by hand. A synthetic T&E dataset of 3,000 expense records is pre-loaded in S3, and a one-command setup step builds the dataset and dashboard for you. The workshop then focuses on Quick's AI features.
Who should take this workshop
This workshop is designed for finance analysts, business intelligence professionals, and data analysts who want to explore AI-powered analytics in Amazon Quick. No coding experience is required. Everything runs in the Console and the Quick UI.

Cost considerations
This workshop uses Amazon Quick (QuickSight) Enterprise edition, which has a monthly subscription cost. In a Workshop Studio event account, the resources are provided at no cost to you. If you run this workshop in your own AWS account, you will incur charges for the Quick subscription, plus minor S3 and Lambda usage. See the Amazon QuickSight pricing page  for details, and complete the Cleanup step to avoid ongoing charges.

What you'll do
Setup (one step): sign up for Quick and run a script that builds the TnE dataset and dashboard automatically.
Agent: build a custom AI agent that answers natural-language questions about the expense data.
Quick Flows: build an AI workflow that turns expense data into an action.
Quick Research: run deep, multi-step analysis over the data.
Quick App: package it into a simple interactive app.
The pre-built foundation
Your setup step produces a TnE Budget vs Actuals dataset and dashboard (spend by category, org, country; budget-vs-actual variance and YTD views). Every AI exercise builds on this same data.

Time and level
Duration: about 60 minutes
Level: 200 (Intermediate)
Prerequisites: none. Everything runs in the AWS Console and the Quick UI.
