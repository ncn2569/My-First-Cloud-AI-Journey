# Summary Report: Amazon Quick & Kiro Fiesta Vietnam 2026 #2

**Date:** June 19, 2026  
**Location:** AWS Vietnam Office (Bitexco Financial Tower, HCMC)  
**Format:** Workshop & Hands-on Lab  

---

### Overview
I attended the morning session of the **"Amazon Quick & Kiro Fiesta Vietnam 2026 #2"**, which focused on "A Day in the Life of a CxO" and the practical applications of AI in finance. Although the event was primarily tailored for C-level executives (CEOs, CIOs, CFOs) and finance professionals, it provided a valuable opportunity to experience cutting-edge AWS AI tools firsthand.

### Morning Session Speakers
- **Miss. Jaye Ding** – Customer Solutions Manager
- **Miss. Sijia Chan** – AWS Finance
- **Mr. Eugene Wong** – AWS Finance

---

### Hands-on Lab: Agentic AI for Finance
The main highlight of my morning was the hands-on lab titled **"Agentic AI for Finance: T&E Analytics with Amazon Quick"**. Working with a synthetic Travel and Expense (T&E) dataset of 3,000 records, I explored the AI capabilities of Amazon Quick without needing to write any code. My activities included:

- **Building a Custom Agent:** Creating an AI agent capable of answering natural-language questions regarding the expense data.
- **Quick Flows:** Designing an AI-driven workflow that turns raw expense data into actionable insights.
- **Quick Research:** Running deep, multi-step analysis across the dataset.
- **Quick App:** Packaging the entire analytical process into a simple, interactive application.

---

### My Experience
While the workshop was not heavily technical and was aimed more at business decision-makers, getting hands-on experience with a brand new AI product like Amazon Quick was quite fascinating. It demonstrated how rapidly AI tools are evolving to democratize data analytics, allowing users to build complex dashboards and automated workflows using simple natural language.
